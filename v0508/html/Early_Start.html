FCD Chinese School
“Early Start” Chinese Learning
At Apple Montessori Schools
 
FCD Chinese School, a well-known local Chinese school, offers “Early Start” Chinese leaning program at Apple Montessori Schools starting from September 2013. FCD Chinese School has offered Chinese programs in the local communities for more than 10 years.
 
Course Goals: The Chinese Learning Program accepts children from 4 to 6 years old. The aim is to help students learn correct pronunciation, rhythm, and dialogue through the interesting and easily memorized materials, to learn key vocabulary words and expressions for daily activities, such as greeting, eating and shopping. With one to two years of learning in pre-K or kindergarten, students will develop a solid foundation for further Chinese study.
 
Course Materials: This program will use Yes Chinese (Langlang Chinese) textbooks. This set of textbooks have been widely used for students who have not a Chinese-speaking environment at home in the United States. It covers from starter level to grade 9. Starter level (and Elementary levels in future) will be used in this program. The basic set includes textbook and workbook. It also offers Flashcards, audio CDs and Online Courses.
 
Teachers: Teachers are strictly selected from FCD Chinese School based on their teaching experience, dedication, Chinese and English language skills and singing and dancing abilities.
 
Locations and Schedule: Convenient locations at Apple Montessori Schools and flexible schedule
