Donations to FCD School
 
FCD Chinese School is a nonprofit Corporation that meets the requirements of section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. The school is exempt from Federal income tax under section 501(c)(3). Contributions to the school are deductible under section 170 of the Code. The school is also qualified to receive tax deductible bequests, devises, transfers or gifts under 2055, 2106 or 2522 of the code. All your contributions are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by the tax law.

FCD Chinese School needs funding in the areas of textbook editing, yearbook publication, teacher professional development, academic support, school rental support, student activities, scholarships for families that are financially challenged, and more.
