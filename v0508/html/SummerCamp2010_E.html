FCD Summer Session  
  Are you designing a unique summer experience for your child? Our Summer Fun Camp provides opportunities for children ages of 4 to 15 to try new activities, make new friends, take on new challenges, and grow in a safe and fun environment.

  
 
  • The session will start on June 21 and end on August 27 in 2010.

  • Convenient location where you can drop and go. The camp is on 334 Plainfield Ave. (Rt.529), near Hwy. 27 in Edison, only 5 minutes walk to Edison train station.

  • Long hours of caring (open at 7:30 and close at 6:30) make it especially suitable for busy working parents.

  • Comprehensive programs offer activities in arts (drama, music, chorus, dance), athletics (basketball, soccer, volleyball), academic (fun Chinese, math, computer, reading, public speaking, journal), and field trips.

  • Warm and caring professional staff; bright and spacious classrooms, hall with stage and safe playgrounds.
