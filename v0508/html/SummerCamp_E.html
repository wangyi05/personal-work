  Fun in the Sun, and Much More!  
  We have the best teachers, 3 top-ranked courses and 8 years of outstanding quality of summer camp.  
  Our summer courses are:  
  English Reading and Writing Course:   starting from English reading, we offer introductory writing and advanced writing courses.  
  Math Intensive Courses:   we focus on cultivating the perception of math and help the campers to improve their ability to solve problems and become good math thinkers.  
  Practical Chinese:   the well-experienced Chinese teacher will cultivate the campers' communicative ability in listening, speaking and reading Chinese by offering creative activities as well as enchanting Chinese Cultural Appreciation Courses.  
  Culture, Sports and Arts Activities:   we focus on well-rounded development by offering a huge selection of courses including Chinese history, Geography, making dumplings, calligraphy, sports, acting, speaking, leadership and talent training on each Friday.  
  Flexible Programs:  
  We offer 3 days and 5 days programs either full day or half day for children from 4-15 years old to suit the need of different families.  
  Please join us, we are open from 7:30am - 6:30 pm, the dates are Jun. 24th to Aug. 30th.  
  This camp is equipped with spacious activity rooms, soccer fields and multimedia facilities to provide enriching sports and culture activities.  
  The whole family is invited to our OPEN HOUSE at 12:30pm-3:30pm, on Mar. 23rd, Mar. 30th and Apr. 13th.
